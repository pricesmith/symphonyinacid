SymphonyinAcid

Max Cooper

This is an interactive website promoting a track Symphony in Acid from the album Unspoken Words by Max Cooper. Animation and code done by Ksawery Komputery.

The official video (Vimeo) features text from Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus (1921) by Ludwig Wittgenstein, dealing with limits of language.

This website is coded entirely in HTML and JavaScript. Press F to enter fullscreen mode or space to pause/play. The content reacts to your mouse and keys Q W E R T.

More about the project: www.ksawerykomputery.pl.
